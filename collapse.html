Bootstrap collapsible

Collapsibles are useful when you want to hide or show large amount of content

Simple collapsible

Click on the button to toggle between showing and hiding content

The .collapse class indicates a collapsible element

This is the content that will be shown or hidden with a click of a button

To control (show/hide) the collapsible content add the data-toggle="collapse" attribute to an <a> or a <button> element

Then add the data-target="#id" attribute to connect the button with the collapsible content
